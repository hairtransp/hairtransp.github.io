Brazilian butt lift in Philadelphia

Philadelphia brazilian butt lift (buttock augmentation) is becoming increasingly popular service in plastic surgery. It is a surgical operation to install the buttock implant. This procedure is performed by both women and men.

Indications

– congenital hypoplasia of the gluteal muscles,
– a small volume of the pelvis (for male physique)
– ptosis of the buttocks,
– sagging skin due to age characteristics, dramatic weight loss,
– uneven distribution of adipose tissue.

The effect from surgery

Getting an attractive and natural shape of the buttocks.
The implant over time does not move and is not damaged.

How to prepare

At the preparatory stage, the most important points is to discuss the desired result, the selection of the implant and the exclusion of contraindications. The main goal of surgery is to obtain a natural harmonious forms in accordance with the general body constitution.Currently available in a wide variety of gluteal implants with a smooth, textured surface, anatomical, rounded, etc.

They have a dense skin to 1000 micrometers thick, filled with cohesive silicone gel. Implants for brazilian butt lift are bio inert: they do not interact with the surrounding tissues. Numerous studies of scientists have shown the safety of silicone. This material does not cause rejection and allergic reactions due to special treatment.

Brazil buttlift Philadelphia is reshaping with the use of fat grafts

Before the procedure is required to complete examination of the patient, it includes:

  1. blood and urine test,
  2. blood biochemistry,
  3. a coagulogram,
  4. electrocardiogram

Operation

Brazilian butt lift in Philadelphia is performed under general anesthesia. 4-5 cm sized incision is performed in the crease between the buttocks, and then forming a pocket implantation. The endoprosthesis is inserted between the large and small gluteal muscles. Then the doctor evaluates the symmetry and the contours of the buttocks, and then sutured to tissue and skin. Incision area is treated with antiseptic. At the end of the operation wear compression stockings.

The recovery period

  1. Recovery takes about 1-2 months.
  2. The patient is in the hospital 1-2 days.
  3. Sutures are removed on 10-14 day .
  4. Soreness concerned for no longer than 2-3 days.
  5. During recovery should limit physical activity.
  6. Within 2 weeks, it is not recommended to sit, sleep on your back, to engage in active sports.

The only restriction after the surgery is the prohibition of an intramuscular injection in the gluteal region.
