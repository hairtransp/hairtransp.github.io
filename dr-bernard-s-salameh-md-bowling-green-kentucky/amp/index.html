Sculptra in the temples?
Correcting volume loss in the temple area
Dec 25, 2017
The temple area is one of the common area missed by injectors. For immediate results fillers made of hyaluronic acid work best. Sculptra works by stimulating your body to make new collagen. It works very well but in needs 2-3 months to get the desired outcome and you might need more than one treatment.
Best of luck. See an expert and a board certified plastic surgeon as this area of the face is trickier than others.
