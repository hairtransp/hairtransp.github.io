I had a Tummy Tuck done at Fairfax Inova Hospital on 5/2015. Would I benefit from abdominal lipo?
Liposuction after Tummy Tuck
Jan 15, 2016
Thank you for your question. These days, liposuction and tummy tucks are more commonly performed at the same time. If you didn't do liposuction with your Tummy Tuck, you definitely can get liposuction performed to create a better curve and shape. Typically, I recommend that a patient wait 6 months after a Tummy Tuck to do liposuction in the abdominal area. Hope this information helps you.
