Is this properly healing?
Wound Healing after Breast Surgery
Dec 26, 2017
Thank you for your question!
It seems that you had breast surgery and the skin was reduced with an inverted T, Wise pattern. It is not uncommon to experience delayed healing at the junction of the vertical and horizontal scar. This is mainly due to tension. Most of the times, these wounds heal by secondary intention, meaning the wound will heal by the bottom upwards.
