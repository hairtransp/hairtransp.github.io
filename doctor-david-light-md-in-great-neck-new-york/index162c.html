What option do I have after a failed DIEP Flap LP Flap?

I'm sorry to hear you have had some difficulties. The only way to know what options are appropriate for you is to do a thorough physical exam. It sounds like you may have had an LD flap, or latissimus dorsi flap, with an implant. This is a common back up plan for a failed DIEP flap reconstruction.
