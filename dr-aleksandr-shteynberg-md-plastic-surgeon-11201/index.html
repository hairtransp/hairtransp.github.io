What can I do for dark skin around the eyes?
dark circles under eyes
Nov 13, 2018
Dark circles under eyes are usually result of fat volume loss in lower eyelids. This creates a separation fold between cheek and eyelid skin. When lower eyelid is deeper than cheek level, light reflection creates appearance of dark circles.
Since the problem is the volume loss usually the treatment is volume restoration with collagen fillers or fat graft. Injection should be done deep to avoid bumps since the eyelid skin is very thin.
